Earth Systems Monitor
Challenges facing our planet are increasingly complex. Threats to natural systems, and to the resources essential to sustaining life, are increasingly under threat. Fortunately, an unprecedented collection of earth observation information is now available to aid in our understanding and advise our actions. The Earth Systems Monitor enables people everywhere to both explore global challenges and to advise meaningful actions to sustain our planet.
For decades, thousands of organizations worldwide have been monitoring and collecting data across our planet that measure human impacts and the pressures against our natural world. These have been brought together in the Earth Systems Monitor, powered by the ArcGIS Living Atlas of the World.
Choose a topic and explore the state of our world to better understand the issues and challenges we face.
